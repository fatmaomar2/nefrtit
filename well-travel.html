Well Travel

Every year many German tourists come to Egypt throughout the year. German tourists visit archaeological sites such as museums and temples, and sometimes they visit natural sites such as reserves and salt lakes[93]. This is because of the ease of travel from Germany to Egypt. A German citizen can obtain an Egyptian visa from the airport or the visa office once he arrives on Egyptian territory[94]. It can also be obtained online by filling out a form and having a current passport. In the visa form, whether online or at the visa office located inside Egyptian airports, some information is required, including the person's name, surname, family name, country of origin and nationality, and information about the passport[95]. After that, he pays 25 US dollars, equivalent to 765,75 Egyptian pounds. Among the airports where visa offices are located are Cairo Airport, Hurghada, and Burj Al Arab in Alexandria[96]. This greatly facilitates the German tourist to visit Egypt anytime as long as his passport is current[97]. As for the Egyptians, it is difficult for them to travel to Germany. This is due to a large number of procedures and security permits for the traveller. In addition, the procedures are carried out between one and six months before the date of travel. Visa fees:[127]

  • The cost of a German visa is 80 euros, which is equivalent to 1545.6 Egyptian pounds[128].

Flight Reservation:[129]

  • Flight reservations start from 629 dollars to 1150 dollars, which is equivalent to 19,266 Egyptian pounds to 35,224 Egyptian pounds[130]. 

Grievance in case of rejection of the visa application:[131]

  • The grievance is made within one month after the rejection decision is issued[132] .
  • The grievance shall be accompanied by documents that were not submitted before or documents that give the applicant an opportunity to reconsider the rejection decision[133] .
  • The grievance letter must contain the rejection letter number, passport number, name, telephone number, or any other means of communication[134] .
  • If the grievance paper is not submitted within a month of the rejection decision, the visa will be submitted again and the fees will be paid again[135] .
  • The grievance can take up to three months[136] .
  • If it is approved after the grievance, the flight ticket cannot be booked until 14 days after the decision[137].
